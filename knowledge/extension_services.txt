SUPPORT SERVICES FOR NIGERIAN FARMERS:

RESEARCH INSTITUTES:
- IITA Ibadan: Cassava, maize, cowpea, soybean research. Free improved varieties.
- NCRI Badeggi: Rice research. FARO varieties.
- NRCRI Umudike: Root and tuber crops (cassava, yam, cocoyam).
- IAR Zaria: Sorghum, millet, groundnut, cotton.
- NIHORT Ibadan: Horticultural crops (fruits, vegetables).

GOVERNMENT PROGRAMMES:
- Agricultural Development Programme (ADP): State-level extension services. Free advisory.
- Anchor Borrowers Programme (CBN): Loans for smallholder farmers through banks.
- NIRSAL: Agricultural credit guarantee. Makes bank loans accessible.
- Fadama Programme: Support for dry season farming. Grants and inputs.

PRIVATE SECTOR:
- AFEX: Commodity exchange and storage. Farmers store and sell when prices are high.
- Babban Gona: Contract farming for maize and rice in the North.
- ThriveAgric: Farm financing and market access.
- Farmcrowdy: Connects farmers to sponsors and markets.
- Hello Tractor: Tractor hiring service via mobile app.
