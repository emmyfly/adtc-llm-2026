POULTRY:
- Broilers: Market ready in 6-8 weeks. Feed cost ~N5,000 per bird to maturity. Selling price N5,500-7,000.
- Layers: Start laying at 18-20 weeks. One bird produces 250-300 eggs per year. Egg price N50-80 each.
- Housing: N3,000-5,000 per bird capacity for deep litter system.
- Feed: Starter (0-4 weeks), grower (4-8 weeks), finisher/layer mash. Buy from Grand Cereals, Vital Feeds, or mix locally.
- Key diseases: Newcastle, Gumboro, coccidiosis. Vaccinate on schedule.

GOAT FARMING:
- West African Dwarf goat: hardy, disease resistant, good for southern Nigeria.
- Red Sokoto: larger, northern Nigeria, good for leather and meat.
- Breeding: 1 buck to 15-20 does. Gestation 150 days. Twins common.
- Market price: N25,000-60,000 per mature goat depending on size and season. Peaks during Sallah.

FISH FARMING (Catfish):
- Most profitable livestock enterprise per square meter.
- Clarias (catfish): Grow to 1kg in 4-6 months. Stocking density 30-50 fish per cubic meter.
- Pond types: Concrete (expensive but durable), tarpaulin (cheaper, N50,000-100,000), earthen (cheapest).
- Feed: 1.5kg feed to produce 1kg fish. Coppens, Durante, or local feed.
- Selling price: N1,500-2,500 per kg depending on size and market.
